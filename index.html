#ThankYouLewis

Lewis Hamilton joined the Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team in 2013, marking the start of one of the most legendary partnerships in the history for Formula 1. With Mercedes F1, Hamilton won seven Drivers' Championships, six of them consecutively, leading to an era of total domination. He broke numerous records, including the most race wins, pole positions, and podium finishes. Hamilton's time at Mercedes has been defined by his consistency, technical skill, and ability to adapt. Beyond the track, he uses his platform to advocate for diversity and social justice, making his mark both within and beyond motorsports.
